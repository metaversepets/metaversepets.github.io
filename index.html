FAQ

Have a question? Please join our Discord to ask more.

What is an NFT?

An NFT stands for “Non-fungible token” which means that it is a unique, and can’t be replaced with something else.

Why did this project start?

This project is inspired by a Youtube video, which is talking about Metaverse. In one of the small pieces of talk, a guest mentioned that you could own a dog in Metaverse if you have owned their NFT. It's inspired me to think about the relationship between the real pets you adopted in the real world and the one in Metaverse. When pets owner loses their pets, it's traumatic for them. It's hard to get over the sadness. But imagine you can still own your pet in Metaverse. You can still have a friendship or be a family together in the Metaverse. You can easily find pets owner who may suffer the same situation you have, and you can help each other to go through the pain. It's the start point when I think about the possibility, which is why I begin this project - Metaverse Pets.

What is the status of this project?

It's still in an early stage, and we are looking for early supporters who can support this project moving to the next step. So if you are interested in this project, please help us by owning one NFT on OpenSea.

How can I claim the free NFT if I have bought one in Early Sale?

You need to hold the NFT until we announce Public Sale.

We will send the free NFT on Ethereum after the Public Sales to the latest holder's address.

How much is the NFT in the Early Sales?

The first 20 NFTs are set at 0.05 ETH, 21~50 NFTs are set at 0.06 ETH, and 51~100 NFTs are set at 0.07 ETH.

How can I enter the whitelist before Public Sales?

It's not decided yet. We will announce the instructions when we sell out 100 NFTs in presale.

Are there royalties?

Yes. There is a 10% royalty (not including OpenSea 2.5% fees) when you sell the NFT. This royalty fee will be contributed back to this project to keep it sustainable for future activities.

What's the Cooperation Plan after Phase 3?

We are open-minded to cooperating with charities or gaming companies. If you are interested in collaborating with us, please contact us by creating a ticket on #open-a-ticket channel in our Disord Server.
